Zero-halogen protection engineered for life.

OPTICAL CLADDING · 125μm

Total internal reflection. Zero signal loss.

125μm diameter silica cladding — the physical law that keeps your signal inside. Every PDR fibre meets ITU-T G.652D and G.657A2 bend-insensitive specifications for passive and active installations.

— ABOUT PDR WORLD

India's fibre optic backbone,
engineered in Mumbai since 1985.

PDR Videotronics India Pvt. Ltd. is one of India's leading manufacturers and suppliers of active and passive fibre optic components and test equipment. We supply major telecom operators, equipment manufacturers, system integrators, defence establishments, ISPs, OEMs, researchers, educational institutions and global distributors — directly from our integrated Mumbai facility. PDR's end users and OEM customers include world leaders in optical communications — a trust that has only deepened over 40 years of consistent delivery. Since 1985, we have invested in product design, development, and manufacturing to remain at the forefront of optical connectivity.

PDR today serves more than 3,000 customers across every segment of the fibre optic industry — from Tier-1 telecommunications carriers and national defence programmes to contractors, resellers, ISPs, original equipment manufacturers, researchers, and government institutions. We are international in scope, supplying customers in more than 15 countries. Our products are available through a growing nationwide network of authorised resellers and distributors.

Why PDR

40+ years of engineering depth. Manufactured under one Mumbai roof.

Most fibre vendors are traders. PDR designs, polishes, terminates, tests and ships from a single integrated facility — giving you genuine accountability, faster turnaround and verifiable quality on every shipment.

01 / Engineering

In-house design & prototyping

Our engineering team designs to your spec — custom polarity, jacket, length, breakout, ferrule type. From first prototype to production run without third-party hand-offs.

Every connector face is end-face inspected and 3D interferometry-tested against IEC standards before it leaves the floor. Optical loss reports ship with every batch.

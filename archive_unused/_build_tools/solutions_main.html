Distributed Sensing & Monitoring

Advanced optical technologies that turn standard fiber cables into continuous sensors for temperature, acoustics, and strain.

Distributed Acoustic Sensing (DAS)

Utilizes fiber optic cables as highly sensitive microphones to detect and locate acoustic vibrations along the entire length of the cable.

  • Pipeline leak detection & intrusion monitoring
  • Railway track and train condition monitoring
  • Perimeter security for high-value assets
Request DAS Specs

Distributed Temperature Sensing (DTS)

Measures temperature profiles continuously along optical fibers, providing pinpoint accuracy over distances up to 50km per channel.

  • Power cable thermal rating & hot spot detection
  • Fire detection in road and rail tunnels
  • Industrial process and storage tank monitoring
